# US-G-028: Six Output & Serial

**Manual Reference:** Section 10.2 & 10.3
**Priority:** Low
**Category:** Auxiliary

## User Story
**As a** operator
**I want** to utilize auxiliary outputs and serial communication
**So that** I can automate processes or log data

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **AC 28.1:** **Six Output (6oP):** Program up to 6 positions to trigger outputs (Pulse or Continuous).
- [ ] **AC 28.2:** **Serial:** Transmit data to PC. Modes: `SEr iAL` (Request) or `SEr Con` (Continuous).
